# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Travelers, local day visitors, families, groups, event planners, and restaurant guests considering Terraza del Pacifico in Playa Hermosa, Costa Rica. They are usually deciding where to stay, dine, celebrate, or spend the day near the beach, often on mobile and often comparing options quickly.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Terraza del Pacifico as a beachfront hotel, restaurant, bar, and event destination. Success means visitors understand the property, trust the experience, and can quickly take the next step: book direct, inquire on WhatsApp, reserve a table, or plan an event.

## Brand Personality

Barefoot, warm, coastal. The brand should feel relaxed and hospitable while still polished enough for weddings, vacations, and special occasions.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic resort templates, stock-feeling luxury, overproduced corporate hospitality, dark teal/green palettes, and text-heavy pages without real property imagery. The design system explicitly avoids green-teal except for the logo artwork and WhatsApp brand color.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the place: real beachfront, pool, dining, and event photography should carry the first impression.
- Make decisions easy: surface prices, schedules, inclusions, and contact actions where visitors need them.
- Stay relaxed but precise: the tone can be warm, but operational details must be clear.
- Keep the brand palette disciplined: ocean blue, gold, sand, and ink are the core materials.
- Design for mobile guests: visitors should be able to scan, ask, and book from a phone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Default to accessible contrast, semantic content structure, keyboard-reachable navigation, descriptive image alt text, and reduced-motion-safe interactions. No additional formal WCAG target has been specified.
